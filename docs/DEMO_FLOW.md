# Recommended Demo Flow

Follow these exact steps to view the optimal "happy path" of ExpenseAutopsy during judging:

1. **Onboarding Context:** Open the app and begin on the onboarding flow. Select a financial goal (e.g., "Financial Freedom").
2. **Dashboard Initialization:** Arrive at the `Dashboard`. Notice the crisp "System Offline" state, indicating readiness to accept incoming user data through AA consent.
3. **Data Fetching:** Click the main call-to-action to link a bank account (Mock AA flow connects "HDFC"). Watch the live terminal-style loaders.
4. **Insights Review:** View the generated dashboard charts and click into "Insights" or scroll to the 5-Year Money Mirror to read the brutal AI feedback about discretionary spending.
5. **Commitment Protocol:** Navigate to the "Commit" page. Experience the Web3 interface and "Stake ETH" (this runs a simulated transaction that automatically clears with a hyper-realistic receipt).
6. **Community Protocol:** Navigate to the "Network" tab to see the live, dynamic leaderboard and activity feed, featuring your newly calculated savings rank among simulated reality peers.
